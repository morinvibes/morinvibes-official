OUR LAND

Bayan Lepas, Penang.

In 2019, we planted the first moringa trees on 2.5 acres of land in Bayan Lepas — a quiet corner of Penang with rich soil and consistent tropical weather.

Today, over 1,200 trees thrive here. No pesticides. No irrigation systems. Just rain, sun, and careful attention from our small team.

Why own the land? Because traceability starts at the root. When you control the source, you control the quality.
