Product & Registration

Is MorinVibes KKM approved? +
Yes! MorinVibes is KKM Approved and GMP Certified. Our facility meets all regulatory requirements of the Ministry of Health Malaysia. Look for the KKM sticker on every bottle.
What is GMP certification? +
Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) ensures products are consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards. It covers all aspects of production from raw materials to finished products — guaranteeing you receive the highest quality moringa.
Are your capsules halal? +
Our capsules are vegetarian (plant-based) and contain no animal products. We are in the process of obtaining halal certification.
Is your moringa organic? +
Our moringa is grown naturally on my family farm without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. I follow traditional farming practices that respect the land. While we're not formally certified organic, our practices align with organic principles.

Suitability & Usage

Who is moringa suitable for? +
Moringa is generally suitable for adults seeking daily nutritional support. If you are pregnant, nursing, taking medication, or have medical conditions, please consult your healthcare provider before use.
How many capsules per day? +
The recommended daily intake is 2 capsules (1g) with water. This can be taken at any time of day, with or without food. Each bottle contains 90 capsules — a 45-day supply.
Can children take moringa? +
We recommend moringa for adults. For children, please consult a pediatrician before use.
What is the shelf life? +
Our capsules have a shelf life of 24 months from the production date. Each bottle shows the current batch date. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
