Ingredients

What's actually in the capsules? +
One ingredient: 100% moringa oleifera leaf powder. No fillers, no binders, no flow agents, no preservatives. The capsule shell is vegetable cellulose (hypromellose), suitable for vegans.
Is it organic? +
While we don't carry formal organic certification (which is expensive and paperwork-heavy for small farms), we grow without any pesticides, herbicides, or synthetic fertilizers. Our moringa is as organic as it gets — just not certified on paper.
Is it vegan / halal / gluten-free? +
Yes to all. Our capsules are plant-based (no gelatin), suitable for vegans. The product is naturally halal and gluten-free. We do not have formal halal certification, but there are no non-halal ingredients or processes involved.
Where do you source your moringa? +
From our own farm in Bayan Lepas, Penang. Every leaf in every capsule comes from trees we planted and tend ourselves. No third-party suppliers, no imported bulk powder.

Usage & Dosage

How many capsules per day and how long does one bottle last? +
3 capsules daily (1.5g) with water, best taken with a meal. 90 capsules = 30-day supply. Consistent daily use is where the difference shows.
When's the best time to take moringa? +
Any time of day, with or after meals. Some people prefer morning for energy, others evening for recovery. The most important thing is consistency — pick a time and stick with it.
Can I open the capsules and mix with food? +
Absolutely. Many people open capsules and mix the powder into smoothies, soups, or yogurt. The taste is earthy and slightly bitter — some love it, some prefer capsules. Either way works.
Is moringa suitable for breastfeeding mothers? +
Moringa has traditionally been used as a galactagogue (milk-flow support) across South and Southeast Asia. Please consult your doctor or lactation consultant before starting any supplement while breastfeeding.
Can children take moringa? +
Moringa is a food, not a medication, and is traditionally eaten as a vegetable. However, for children under 12, we recommend consulting with a healthcare provider for appropriate dosage.
