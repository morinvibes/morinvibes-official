The Process

01

Harvest

I harvest at sunrise when moringa leaves are at their peak nutrient density. Leaves are stripped from stems by hand and processed the same morning — no cold storage, no delays between picking and processing.

6:00 AM harvest Same-day processing Hand-picked

02

Dry

Leaves are dried using a gentle low-temperature method that protects the natural phytonutrients, enzymes, and chlorophyll. This step is what separates quality moringa from mass-produced powder — no high heat, no shortcuts.

Low-temperature Nutrient preserved

03

Mill

Dried leaves are milled at low speed into a fine, silky powder. Nothing added. No fillers, no anti-caking agents, no flow aids — just pure moringa leaf at its most natural state.

Fine powder Zero additives

04

Capsule

Pure powder is filled into vegetarian capsules — 500mg each, 90 per bottle. No binders, no stearates, no silica. Just moringa leaf and a plant-based capsule shell. Sealed and labelled by hand before shipping.

500mg each 90 capsules Vegetarian shell KKM Approved
