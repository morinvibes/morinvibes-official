Bayan Lepas, Penang

Our farm sits on 2.5 acres of land in Bayan Lepas, Penang — a stone's throw from our encapsulation facility. This proximity is deliberate: leaves go from branch to bottle in hours, not days or weeks.

We planted the first moringa trees in 2019. Today, over 1,200 trees thrive here, their roots deep in Malaysian soil, their leaves harvested at peak nutrition.

No middlemen. No imported powder. No warehouse stock sitting for months. Just our land, our trees, our harvest.

HOW WE GROW

Chemical-Free, Always.

Healthy soil grows nutrient-dense leaves. It's that simple.

💧
Rain-Fed Only

No irrigation systems — our trees drink only natural rainwater, deepening their root systems.

🌱
No Chemicals

Zero pesticides, herbicides, or synthetic fertilisers. Just compost and natural soil health.

🌿
Hand-Harvested

Every leaf is hand-picked at peak maturity — machines damage leaves and reduce nutrient density.

🌞
Morning Harvest

Leaves picked at dawn when nutrient and moisture content is highest — processed within hours.

FROM TREE TO CAPSULE

The Same Day.

Harvest. Clean. Dry. Encapsulate. All within hours — not weeks or months.

STEP 1 · DAWN
Hand Harvest

Leaves are hand-picked at first light, selecting only the most mature, nutrient-dense foliage.

STEP 2 · MORNING
Gentle Washing

Leaves are rinsed in cool, filtered water to remove any natural debris — no chemicals, no heat.

STEP 3 · MIDDAY
Low-Temperature Drying

Dried at temperatures below 40°C to preserve chlorophyll, enzymes, and delicate nutrients.

STEP 4 · AFTERNOON
Stone-Ground Milling

Dried leaves are ground into fine powder using traditional stone mills — no heat generation.

STEP 5 · EVENING
Encapsulation

Fresh powder is encapsulated in our GMP-certified facility — sealed in bottles the same day.

From our farm to your door — one origin, one ingredient, one promise.
