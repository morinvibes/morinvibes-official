Our Story

Penang-Grown.
Honestly Built.

We started in 2019 the way most honest things start — by hand, in Penang. Every moringa plant was grown by us. Every capsule packed by us. Before factory equipment existed, we submitted our powder to independent laboratory testing at Universiti Sains Malaysia — because we needed to know, and our customers deserved to know.

In 2025, we built our own purpose-built production facility in Bayan Lepas — semi-automated encapsulation, precision weighing to exactly 500mg, and a team trained to GMP standards.

In 2026, we achieved KKM traditional medicine registration and GMP certification from Malaysia's Ministry of Health — the formal recognition of a standard we had been maintaining, quietly, since day one.
