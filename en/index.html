How do I know this is genuinely KKM approved? +
MorinVibes® holds a valid KKM registration and GMP certification issued by the Ministry of Health Malaysia. Both are publicly verifiable — see our Quality & Authenticity page for registration numbers and documentation.

Recommended: 3 capsules daily (1.5g) with water, best taken with meals. Each bottle holds 90 capsules — a precise 30-day supply. Consistent daily use is where you'll notice a difference.

Yes. East Malaysia shipping is RM15. West Malaysia is RM7. Buying 10 bottles? You get 1 free — WhatsApp us to arrange.
Is buying direct different from Shopee or Lazada? +
Same certified product, same batch. Direct orders through our website go straight to us — no marketplace cut means we can offer better bundles and personalised service. Choose whatever channel you're most comfortable with.
Is moringa suitable for me? +
Generally suitable for adults as a daily food supplement. If you are pregnant, nursing, managing a medical condition, or on regular medication, consult your healthcare provider before starting any supplement.
