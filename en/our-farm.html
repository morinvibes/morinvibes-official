Where Leaves Become Capsules

Our facility is designed for gentle processing. Leaves are dried at low temperatures, milled, and encapsulated within the same building — preserving the vibrant green colour and nutrition.

Every batch is logged, every capsule counted. We maintain GMP standards not just on paper, but in daily practice.
