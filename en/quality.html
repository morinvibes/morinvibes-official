TRUST BUT VERIFY

How to Verify Our Certifications.

Don't take our word for it. Check the official government database yourself.

STEP 1
Visit NPRA Official Website

Go to www.npra.gov.my — the National Pharmaceutical Regulatory Agency portal.

STEP 2
Navigate to Product Search

Click on "Product Search" or "Carian Produk" under the "QUEST" database section.

STEP 3
Enter Registration Number

Type our KKM registration number: MAL12345678T (replace with actual number when available).

STEP 4
Verify Product Details

The database will show our product name, manufacturer, and certification status — all matching our label.

STEP 5
Check GMP Status

Our facility is listed in the GMP database separately. Search by company name "MorinVibes" to confirm.
