Common Questions About Returns

Can I return if I don't like the taste? +
Moringa has a naturally earthy, slightly bitter taste — it's a sign of potency, not a defect. Because of hygiene regulations, we can't accept returns for opened products based on taste preference. However, if the taste seems off (e.g., stale or rancid), that could indicate a quality issue — please contact us.
What if my package was stolen? +
Once the tracking shows "delivered," the package is the responsibility of the carrier. Please contact the courier directly to file a claim. If you have video evidence or a police report, we can assist in communicating with the courier.
Do you refund shipping costs? +
If we determine the damage or defect was our fault, we will refund the full amount including shipping. If the issue is with the courier (e.g., lost package), we'll work with you to file a claim.
How long do refunds take? +
Once approved, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. Depending on your bank, it may take additional time to appear in your account.
