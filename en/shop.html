100% Pure Moringa Leaf Powder

Our capsules contain exactly one ingredient: moringa oleifera leaf powder, grown on our own farm in Penang. No fillers. No binders. No flow agents. Just the leaf, gently dried and encapsulated.

Each capsule delivers 500mg of pure moringa — 3 capsules provide a 1.5g daily serving. This is the same dosage traditionally used in Southeast Asian communities for daily nutrition.

Why whole-leaf powder? Because isolating individual nutrients loses the synergistic effect. The leaf works as a complete food, not a synthetic isolate.

One Ingredient Only

Moringa oleifera leaf powder (100%) — that's it.

The capsule shell is made from vegetable cellulose (hypromellose), suitable for vegans and vegetarians.

Free from: gluten, soy, dairy, nuts, preservatives, artificial colours, artificial flavours, and GMOs.
