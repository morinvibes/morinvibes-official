THE BEGINNING

2019: A Personal Journey

My body was often weak. Discovering moringa changed my daily energy and became part of my life journey.

It started in my house compound, on my own land in Kampung Balik Pulau. I was struggling, and moringa became my lifeline. I grew it myself, harvested it myself, processed it myself — from A to Z, done by my own hands. It was difficult, but it gave me purpose.

Today, I am still a farmer. I own the land. There are no middlemen. Every leaf is harvested by my hands, gently dried, and prepared with purpose. This is not a business built by investors — it's a life built by a farmer who found hope in a plant.

"I started small, with one harvest and a simple goal — prepare moringa the way it deserves." — Jess, Founder
THE JOURNEY

From Garden to Farm

By 2020, what started as a backyard garden became my family farm in the hills of Balik Pulau. I tend the land myself, with no middlemen — just me, growing moringa the way it should be grown.

Today, my farm supplies all our moringa directly. Every leaf is harvested by hand, dried with care, and prepared with purpose.
