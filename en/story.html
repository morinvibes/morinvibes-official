The Beginning

How It All Started

My body was often weak. Discovering moringa changed my daily energy and became part of my life journey. It wasn't a business idea at first — it was survival, then purpose.

It started in my house compound, on my own land in Kampung Balik Pulau. I grew it myself, harvested it myself, processed it myself — from A to Z, done by my own hands. It was difficult, but it gave me purpose.

What began as a small garden became a farm. What began as personal healing became a mission to share. In 2019, I opened my first Shopee store. Word spread — through family, friends, then strangers who felt the difference.

Today, I am still a farmer. I own the land. There are no middlemen. Every leaf is harvested by my hands, gently dried, and prepared with purpose. This is not a business built by investors — it's a life built by a farmer who found hope in a plant.
