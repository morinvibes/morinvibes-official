COMMON QUESTIONS

Quick Answers

Find answers to the most common questions about MorinVibes, our moringa, and your order.

Expand All

Product & Registration

Is MorinVibes registered with KKM? +
Registration is currently in progress. Our facility follows GMP standards and we maintain full transparency about our process. We are working closely with NPRA to complete the registration.
What is GMP certification? +
Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) ensures our products are consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards. It covers all aspects of production from raw materials to finished products.
Are your capsules halal? +
Our capsules are vegetarian (plant-based) and contain no animal products. We are in the process of obtaining halal certification.

Suitability & Usage

Who is moringa suitable for? +
Moringa is generally suitable for adults seeking daily nutritional support. If you are pregnant, nursing, taking medication, or have medical conditions, please consult your healthcare provider before use.
How many capsules per day? +
The recommended daily intake is 2 capsules (1g) with water. This can be taken at any time of day, with or without food.
Can children take moringa? +
We recommend moringa for adults. For children, please consult a pediatrician before use.
What is the shelf life? +
Our capsules have a shelf life of 24 months from the production date. Each package shows the batch number and preparation date.

Shipping & Delivery

Shipping to East Malaysia? +
Yes, we ship to Sabah and Sarawak. Delivery typically takes 3-5 business days for West Malaysia, 5-7 days for East Malaysia.
How much is shipping? +
Shipping is free for West Malaysia. For East Malaysia (Sabah/Sarawak), shipping is RM15.
How soon do you ship? +
We prepare fresh batches upon order. Shipping within 24-48 hours of order confirmation.
Do you ship internationally? +
Currently, we only ship within Malaysia. We hope to offer international shipping in the future.

Shopee & Official Store

How is this different from Shopee sellers? +
We are the original farm source since 2019. Shopee resellers purchase from us or others — buying directly ensures you receive the freshest batch with full traceability. Our official website offers fresher batches and direct founder support.
What are your Shopee store IDs? +
Our official Shopee stores are: MorinVibes and moringa_morinvibes. We also have a presence on Lazada.
Why buy from your website instead of Shopee? +
Buying from our official website guarantees the freshest batch (prepared after your order), direct founder support, and early access to new products like our upcoming moringa tea.

Orders & Returns

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and FPX through our secure Orderla.my checkout.
Can I cancel or change my order? +
Please contact us within 2 hours of placing your order if you need to make changes. Once preparation begins, changes may not be possible.
What is your return policy? +
Due to the nature of our fresh preparation process, we cannot accept returns. However, if you receive a damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours with photos, and we will make it right.
How can I track my order? +
After your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp or email, depending on your contact preference during checkout.

Product Details

What are the ingredients? +
100% Moringa oleifera leaf powder in vegetarian capsules. No fillers, no additives, no preservatives.
How many capsules per bottle? +
Each bottle contains 60 vegetarian capsules, with 500mg of pure moringa powder per capsule — a one-month supply at the recommended daily intake of 2 capsules.
Is it organic? +
Our moringa is grown naturally without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. We follow traditional farming practices that align with organic principles.
Is it caffeine-free? +
Yes, moringa is naturally caffeine-free. It's suitable for any time of day.
