02 — DRYING

Shade-Dried.
Nutrient Protected.

We never use heat drying. Leaves rest in shaded, ventilated racks — a slower process that preserves the natural compounds. Drying takes 3-5 days, depending on the weather.

Why shade drying matters
  • Preserves nutrients — No heat damage
  • Natural process — 3-5 days slow drying
  • Temperature — Never exceeds 35°C
03 — MILLING

Gentle Milling.
Nothing Added.

Dried leaves are milled at low speed to preserve texture and purity. The powder is then encapsulated — no fillers, no flow agents, just pure moringa.
