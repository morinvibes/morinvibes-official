02 — DRYING

Shade-Dried.
Nutrient Protected.

We never use heat drying. Leaves rest in shaded, ventilated racks — a slower process that preserves the natural compounds. Drying takes 3-5 days, depending on the weather.

✓ Preserves nutrients — No heat damage
✓ Natural process — 3-5 days slow drying

03 — MILLING

Gentle Milling.
Nothing Added.

Dried leaves are milled at low speed to preserve texture and purity. The powder is then encapsulated — no fillers, no flow agents, just pure moringa from our family farm.
