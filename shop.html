Farm-Direct Moringa from Penang

MorinVibes Moringa capsules are exactly what the name suggests — pure moringa leaf powder from our farm in Penang, carefully prepared and encapsulated. No extracts, no concentrates, no fillers. Just the leaf, dried gently and milled finely.

Each capsule contains 500mg of pure moringa powder. The recommended daily intake is 2 capsules (1g), providing a simple way to incorporate moringa into your daily wellness routine.

Unlike mass-produced supplements that may sit in warehouses for months, our batches are prepared after you order. From harvest to your door, the journey takes approximately 7-10 days — ensuring you receive the freshest possible product.
