Farm-Direct Moringa from Penang

MorinVibes Moringa capsules are exactly what the name suggests — pure moringa leaf powder from our family farm in Balik Pulau, Penang, carefully prepared and encapsulated. No extracts, no concentrates, no fillers. Just the leaf, dried gently and milled finely.

Each bottle contains 90 vegetarian capsules with 500mg of pure moringa powder per capsule. The recommended daily intake is 2 capsules (1g), providing a simple way to incorporate moringa into your daily wellness routine.
