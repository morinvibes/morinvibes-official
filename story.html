THE BEGINNING

2019: A Nurse Mother's Wisdom

In difficult years, my mother — a nurse — introduced me to moringa. She had seen its traditional use in the community and believed in its gentle daily support.

What began as a simple family remedy grew into something larger. Friends started asking. Then strangers. We were harvesting from our small garden in Balik Pulau, but the demand kept growing.

"We started small, with one harvest and a simple goal — prepare moringa the way it deserves."

— Founder, MorinVibes

THE JOURNEY

From Garden to Family Farm

By 2020, what started as a backyard garden became our family farm in the hills of Balik Pulau. We tend the land ourselves, with no middlemen — just our family, growing moringa the way it should be grown.

Today, our farm supplies all our moringa directly. Every leaf is harvested by hand, dried with care, and prepared with purpose.
